FAQ

Common Questions

Have questions before reaching out? Here are answers to the most common things clients ask before their first consultation.

View All Services

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes - completely. The initial consultation (up to 45 minutes) is offered at no charge. There's no obligation to retain our services afterward. It's an opportunity to understand your situation and explore how we can help.

Can I consult via video call?

Absolutely. We offer virtual consultations via Zoom or Google Meet for clients who prefer to meet remotely or are located outside Hyderabad. All platforms used are secure and encrypted.

What documents should I bring?

For the first consultation, simply bring any documents you feel are relevant - marriage certificate, previous court orders, property documents, or existing wills. Don't worry if you don't have everything; we'll guide you on what to gather afterward.

How are legal fees structured?

Fees are discussed transparently during the consultation and depend on the nature and complexity of your matter. We offer retainer-based arrangements for ongoing matters and flat-fee services for document drafting (wills, trusts). There are no hidden charges - ever.

Is my information confidential?

100%. All information shared with Attorney Reddy - from the first contact through case completion - is protected by attorney-client privilege and our strict confidentiality policy. It will never be disclosed to any third party without your explicit consent.
