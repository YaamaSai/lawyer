In Focus

Estate Planning That Endures

A well-crafted estate plan is one of the most important gifts you can give your family. Attorney Reddy designs plans that minimize tax burden, avoid probate delays, and ensure your exact wishes are carried out.

Do I need a trust if I already have a will?

While a will is essential, a trust offers additional benefits - avoiding probate, providing for minor children, managing assets if you become incapacitated, and offering tax advantages. Many comprehensive estate plans include both.

What happens if I die without a will in India?

If you die intestate (without a will), your assets are distributed according to the Hindu Succession Act or the Indian Succession Act - which may not align with your wishes. This can cause family disputes and costly probate proceedings.

How often should I update my estate plan?

Review your estate plan after major life events: marriage, divorce, birth of a child, significant asset changes, or the death of a beneficiary. We recommend a full review every 3-5 years regardless.
